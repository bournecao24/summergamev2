How to Play

Getting started with Summer Games is easy! Simply use the game controls to navigate through the various challenges and activities:

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character
  • Press Space to jump or interact with objects
  • Collect items to increase your score and unlock new levels
  • Complete challenges within the time limit to earn bonus points

The game automatically saves your progress, so you can return anytime to continue your summer adventure!
